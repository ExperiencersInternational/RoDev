RoDev is a free online opensource learning platform for anybody to use. With working tutorials that often contain an interactive powerpoint/video, RoDev is the learning platform for you. Made by Flynn Farrow (GamersInternational). Project inspired by Lua Learning. The project is also being coded by hand for now however I hope to keep the quality a very high quality still. Pages may not be optimised for mobile, I don't generally know how to develop well enough to make it work great on both desktop and mobile (it's working fine on both at the time of writing).
